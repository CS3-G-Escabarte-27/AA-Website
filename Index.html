The Terraria Experience

Traveler, welcome to the world of Terraria! Here you will fight monsters of the night, explore the vast corners of the world, and defeat the strongest of bosses. Become a master swordsman, with unwavering dedication, great physical strength, and a deep understanding of the blade, honed through hours of practice and training. A ranger, dedicates his life to gain mastery over mental focus and precision, able to hit a target from anywhere imaginable. Perhaps being mage could suit you, capable of manipulating the mana within you, and turn that mana into your power. Learn the secret to life, and make your own through the use of mana, capable of controlling and manipulating the life you created to your will.
